# 📈 Crypto Market Sentiment vs Trader Performance Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes the relationship between cryptocurrency market sentiment and trader performance using historical trading records and the Fear & Greed Index.

The objective is to investigate whether trader behavior and profitability change under different market sentiment conditions and to identify trading patterns that can support better decision-making.

---

## Dataset

Two datasets were used:

### 1. Historical Trading Data
Contains information about:
- Trader accounts
- Trade direction
- Position size
- Profit & Loss (PnL)
- Trading fees
- Execution timestamps

### 2. Fear & Greed Index
Contains:
- Daily market sentiment
- Fear & Greed score
- Sentiment classification

## Key Findings

- Traders generated the highest average daily profits during Extreme Fear market conditions.
- High-frequency traders achieved significantly higher average total profits.
- Consistent winners executed more trades while maintaining higher win rates.
- Market sentiment influences both trader behavior and trading performance.
